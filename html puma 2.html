This fresh take on the classic Suede draws inspiration from PUMA’s
heritage within breakdancing and its influence on modern streetwear.
The Suede XL retains the Suede’s iconic DNA,
but gives it a twist with an exaggerated padded collar and tongue, plus a chunkier sole.
This execution features a full suede upper with a leather Formstrip.
Puma Suede Features:
  • suede upper
  • Leather Formstrip.
  • Suede tongue with mesh bending.
  • Debossed and foil-printed PUMA branding on quarter panel
  • Mesh lining.
  • Mesh sockliner
  • Rubeer outsole.
  • Rubber midsole.
