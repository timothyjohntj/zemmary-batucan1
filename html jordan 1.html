The first Air Jordan shoe was produced for basketball player Michael Jordan during his time
with the Chicago Bulls on November 17, 1984, and released to the public on April 1, 1985.
The shoes were designed for Nike by Peter Moore, Tinker Hatfield, and Bruce Kilgore.
Jordan 1 Features:
  • A rubber midsole and outsole for sturdiness and weight.
  • An Air-Sole unit for lightweight cushioning.
  • A textile tongue for comfort.
  • A solid rubber outsole for traction on various surfaces.
  • Genuine leather in the upper for durability and a premium look.
