The Air Jordan 2 is a classic basketball sneaker from the legendary Air Jordan line by Nike,
originally released in 1986. Designed by Bruce Kilgore and produced by Nike,
it was the second signature shoe of Michael Jordan and represents a shift in basketball footwear design,
combining luxury materials with performance features. Here's a detailed look at the Air Jordan 2
Jordan 2 Features:
  • A rubber midsole and outsole for sturdiness and weight.
  • An Air-Sole unit for lightweight cushioning.
  • A textile tongue for comfort.
  • A solid rubber outsole for traction on various surfaces.
  • Genuine leather in the upper for durability and a premium look.
